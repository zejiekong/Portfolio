Bike Shelter

For this group project, my team ideated a foldable bike shelter to shelter food delivery riders from harsh weather. As an active team member, I was heavily involved in all stages of the design and prototyping process. Some of my personal contributions include providing a rough sketch of the bike shelter to aid the protoyping process. Besides that, I also gained substantial hands-on experience in the prototyping stage as multiple power tools were used (e.g. drill gun, rivet ...) Lastly, I also took initiative to cover the more technical aspects of our business report (Financial Plan). Takeaway from this project: In order for a group project to be successful, everyone on the team should be on the same page and have at least some level of enthusiasm. If that is not possible, focus on what you can do for the team instead. "You can't change other people. You can only change yourself." - Lorraine Bracco
