Automatic Shirt Folder

An automatic shirt folder machine ,targeting the issues that blind people face when they are folding their clothes. This project was challenging because of time constraints. We had to complete our prototype within less than a month. Nevertheless, I took on the role of a leader. As I wanted to get the most out of this experience, I took every opportunity to learn from my group members when they are carrying out their assigned tasks. Given my limited leadership skills, I had to learn how to be a better leader along the way. Most of the time, I would keep track on everyone's progress and if any team members face problems, I would then help to address them. Of course as a leader's responsibility, I also had to get parts from lab assistants and also seek help from them whenever there's a need for that. Last but not least, I was also the presenter for my group's prototype :) My biggest takeaway from this project would be : How to be objective and not let personal feelings get in the way of work, especially when receiving or giving feedback.
